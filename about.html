"Hello! Introducing to the world"

Tibia Home Decoration Page 2022

Our taste for house decoration begins in 2007, when we started playing the game. Many people disliked the idea of using a MMORPG game to just decorate houses!

Altough many people disliked what we did, many other started to support us, as they also shared the love with house decoration, wich made us gain more and more recognition inside the game and withing the community

Post COVID-19

During the pandemic, many people returned to the game seeking a way to spend time while staying at home

At the same time, together with some friends, we created a group to gather as many decoration lovers as possible to invent new decoration styles.

Now, we are developing a community with over a hundred players from all over the globe that help us with ideas and items needed to make the most creative decorations ever seen!
